How to Play

Welcome to the Word Similarity Game! Your goal is to clear the board by finding hidden connections between words.

The Rules:

  • Match Words: Type a word or a short sentence that relates to one or more words currently on the screen. The closer the meaning, the better the match!
  • Score Points: You earn points for every successful match. Matching multiple words at once with a clever phrase gives you a massive combo bonus!
  • Beat the Clock: Keep an eye on the timer. Every matched word adds +2 seconds to your clock! Clear as many words as you can before time runs out to secure a high score.
  • Use Shuffle: Stuck? Use the Shuffle button to rearrange the words and spot new connections.

Ready to test your vocabulary and associative thinking? Play now and see how high you can score!

## 🎮 Game Overview

Dive into a world of semantic puzzles where your goal is to uncover hidden
words using hints provided by a powerful AI.

- **Play Anywhere**: Seamlessly switch between the sleek **Web interface** and the **Telegram Bot**.
- **PvP Challenges**: Invite friends to a duel! Challenge links ensure you both face the **exact same words**, creating a fair and competitive environment.
- **Score Sharing**: Brag about your best streaks and high scores with beautiful, shareable scorecards.

---

## ⚔ Features

- **Seeded Gameplay**: Challenges use matching seeds so players encounter the identical sequence of words and refills.
- **Multilingual Support**: Play in English, German, or Russian.
- **Real-time Feedback**: Instant similarity calculations powered by advanced language models.
- **Bonus Time**: Every correct guess adds time to your clock - how long can you survive?
